3. Data Handling

Data persists only within the local database of your installation and is purged only upon your explicit request.

4. Professional Responsibility
You acknowledge that you are a qualified professional authorised to access and analyse the financial materials being uploaded. You accept full responsibility for ensuring appropriate consent, authority, and legal basis exists for you to process the documents submitted to this platform.

LexCrypta LLC makes no warranty as to the completeness of findings and accepts no liability for reliance on Evaluate outputs without independent verification by a qualified professional.
