1. Authorized Access Only

LexCrypta Verify is provided exclusively to licensed legal professionals, certified accountants, court-appointed trustees, and authorized personnel of their firms. Access by any other party is strictly prohibited. By proceeding, you confirm you are an authorized user operating within your professional capacity.

2. Purpose of Use

This platform is a forensic financial analysis tool designed to assist professionals in identifying financial anomalies, asset patterns, and evidentiary signals within bank statement data. All analysis outputs are provided for informational purposes to support professional judgment. Nothing produced by this platform constitutes legal advice, accounting advice, or a final determination of fact.

3. Data Handling & Storage

Documents uploaded to LexCrypta Verify are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections and processed on secure cloud infrastructure hosted in the United States (Railway). Matter data, extracted transactions, and analysis results are stored in an encrypted PostgreSQL database (Supabase) accessible only to authorized users of your licensed deployment. Lexcrypta LLC does not access, review, or share your uploaded documents or client data with any third party.
